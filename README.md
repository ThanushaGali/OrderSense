# 🍽️ OrderSense AI  
### Know the wait before you order.

OrderSense AI is an intelligent food delivery assistant designed to help users make smarter ordering decisions by understanding restaurant demand and delivery reliability before placing an order. It provides clear insights into how busy restaurants are and highlights faster options, helping users avoid unnecessary delays and improve their overall ordering experience.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Food delivery has become an essential part of daily life, but users often experience unexpected delays due to crowded kitchens, peak-hour demand, and high order volumes. Although delivery platforms display estimated times, they do not provide enough visibility into real-time restaurant workload or reliability.

This lack of transparency makes it difficult for users to choose the best option, leading to frustration, wasted time, and reduced satisfaction. There is a growing need for a solution that helps users understand demand conditions and make faster, more informed ordering decisions.

---

## 💡 Solution

OrderSense AI introduces a smarter way to approach food ordering by providing visibility into restaurant demand and delivery conditions before an order is placed.

The system helps users:

- Identify busy restaurants in advance  
- Understand expected delivery delays  
- Discover faster and more reliable options  
- Make confident ordering decisions  
- Improve overall delivery experience
  
By offering simple and clear insights, the solution reduces uncertainty and helps users save time.


---


## 🖥️ Features

- Provides visibility into restaurant demand levels  
- Highlights delivery delay risks  
- Suggests faster alternatives to reduce waiting time  
- Offers an interactive assistant for quick guidance  
- Displays demand insights through a simple dashboard  
- Helps users make better and quicker decisions  
- Supports search and easy navigation

## 📊 How It Works

1. The user searches for a restaurant or requests assistance  
2. The system evaluates restaurant demand and delivery conditions  
3. Potential delay risks are identified  
4. Faster or more reliable options are suggested
